About Me

Let me introduce myself, Montreal born world traveler and you can me Vana which stuck with me since high school. I've been a major fashionista since the late 90s. My passion is fashion and it would make sense that I ended up opening my own luxury boutique. Presentation Presentation Presention !! Can't say it enough.

I've been blessed to work in an industry that im really passionate about which might be a shocker! The last 8 years ive had the opportunity to make a difference in the high risk youth sector and I myself have noticed alot of these youths have low self esteem due to many factors. My first line of action was when you look good you feel good. That alone has a major impact on any person self esteem. Although esthetics might make a child feel better there obviously is more involved but self esteem is defitnely a step in the right direction.

Ten Percent of the proceeds from this boutique goes towards black and brown youth coalition.
